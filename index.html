About Hack4Cause

IEEE-SSIT would like to bring Hack4Cause, a 24-hours hackathon which aims to bring creativity and innovation among students. Hack4Cause bringds students from different domains of engineering together to build, design and use the latest technology to hack into real-world problems. The dates for the event are the 10th and 11th of March. The main motive behind Hack-4-Cause is to bring a change in the thinking perspective of students in critical industrial situations. As the students will be solving industry-based problems, they will get to know about the technologies involved in leading industries. This event will not only bridge the gap between theoretical and practical knowledge but at the same time will motivate students to take challenges and come out with an innovative solution to these challenges.
